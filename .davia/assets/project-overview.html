Project Overview

People In Axis is an enterprise HR and talent management platform.

Combines employee management, time tracking, expense management, and recruitment in one unified system.

Key Capabilities:

  • Complete employee lifecycle management
  • Advanced timesheet tracking with approval workflows
  • Expense management and reimbursement processing
  • Recruitment and talent acquisition system
  • Project tracking and resource allocation
  • Multi-company data isolation
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Comprehensive audit logging
