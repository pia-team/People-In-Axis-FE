Timesheet Module

Time tracking and approval workflows.

Features:

  • Weekly/monthly timesheet creation
  • Project-based time tracking
  • Approval workflow (Team Manager → HR)
  • Timesheet history tracking
  • Excel import/export

Status Flow:

  • Draft → Submitted → Pending Team Manager
  • Approved by Team Manager → Pending HR
  • Final Approved → Completed

Access:

  • Employees: Create own timesheets
  • Team Managers: Approve team timesheets
  • HR: Approve all timesheets
